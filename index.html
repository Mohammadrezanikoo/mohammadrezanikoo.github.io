About Dr. Mohammad Reza Nikoo

Dr. Mohammad Reza Nikoo is an Associate Professor at Sultan Qaboos University, Oman (2021–present), and a member of the Iranian Federation of Young Elites (2008–present). He received the 2024 Oman National Research Award and was ranked among the world’s top 2% scientists by Stanford University. He is also the top-ranked Civil Engineer in Oman (2024). Dr. Nikoo has served as an Honorary Visiting Fellow at the University of Technology Sydney (2022–present). Formerly at Shiraz University (2017–2021), he was Iran’s Eminent Young Scientist (2020). He has supervised 12 Ph.D. and 45 MSc theses and published 190+ papers with 5961 citations (H-index: 42).
